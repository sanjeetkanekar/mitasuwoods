We Start Where It Matters: Kiln-Seasoned Wood

Because true craftsmanship begins with choosing the right foundation.

Wood is more than just a material at Mitasu; it is the essence of our trade. Because raw or air-dried wood is more likely to warp, crack, or rot over time, we never use it. We use kiln-seasoned wood instead, which has been expertly dried in controlled chambers to attain the ideal moisture balance.

The Significance of Kiln Seasoning

Kiln seasoning ensures that every piece of wood used in your furniture is crafted to last a lifetime.

Dimensional Stability

No unforeseen seasonal shrinkage or enlargement.

Resistance to Insects & Fungi

Eliminates internal decay threats and pests.

Warp-Free Longevity

Maintains strength and alignment for decades.

Improved Finishing

Joinery, paints, and polishes adhere perfectly.

This extra step, often skipped by many, is our way of ensuring that your furniture stays as reliable as it is beautiful. Kiln-seasoning is not an add-on, it’s our standard.
