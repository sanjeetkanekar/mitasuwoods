Why Mitasu ?

Created with purpose. Designed to Last.

In a world where fast furniture is common, Mitasu takes a different route.

We're not interested in mass-producing identical parts or taking shortcuts to increase volume. Furniture that tells your story, is what Mitasu is all about. Each piece is created with intention, ethically crafted by hand, and built to last for many generations.
